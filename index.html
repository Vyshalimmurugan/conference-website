About our Institution

Our institution is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) in New Delhi and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. It is also NAAC-accredited. Offering eight undergraduate programs in engineering and applied sciences, our college ranks among the top self-financing engineering institutions in Tamil Nadu, renowned for its academic excellence, cutting-edge facilities, consistent track record of outstanding results, and nurturing enterprising graduates.

About the Conference


The International Conference on Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (ICAIDS-24) is a prestigious event designed to provide an exceptional international platform for academicians, researchers, engineers, industrial professionals and budding students worldwide to share their research findings with leading global experts. Submissions will be carefully assessed based on their originality, technical and/or research content/depth, accuracy, relevance to the conference theme, contributions to the field, and clarity of writing.
